Vendor Prefixes

Keyframe Animation Syntax with vendor prefixes.

A way for web browser developers and designers to experiment with new features before they are formalized by the w3c. More on vendor prefixes. Instead of writing them by hand you can use many other techniques such as pre-processors: LESS (lessprefixer) and Sass, autoprefixer, GRUNT and more.
