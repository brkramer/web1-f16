Homework #6 —Screen Reading

  • Course Home

Verify your Event Promotion page is usable using a screen reader of your choosing. Due by the start of next class.

Goals

  1. Practice using a screen reader to gain confidence testing with it on future projects.
  2. Gain an understanding of how one can change HTML to ease screen reader use.

Deliverables

  1. Create a list of use cases for your event promotion page.
  2. Using a screen reader attempt to achieve one of these use cases. Document the steps you took including what you listened to and what key-presses were required.
  3. Take a look at the list you have created. Are there too many steps? Confusing steps? Is the use case even achievable? Update your HTML to resolve issues. See creating accessible websites tutorial.
  4. Using a screen reader attempt to achieve this use case again. Document again. Did you improve the workflow or reduce the amount of steps?
  5. Record all of your documentations on your Student Page, or create a new page for this documentation.

Revisions

The following are suggested incremental steps towards moving your projects forward. They do not have a fixed due date, manage your time appropriately.

  1. For your Client Site revise your wireframes, mockups and Style Tiles based on class and client feedback as needed. Email, Skype or meet with your client for additional feedback.
  2. For your Client Site collect all site content including items such as about page text, intro text, employee bios, description of services, photos, contact information and whatever else is needed.
